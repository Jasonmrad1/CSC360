Biography

I was born and raised in Ghazir, Lebanon. From a young age, I developed a keen interest in technology and computers, which eventually led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. I graduated from Ghazir Public School as the valedictorian and achieved high distinction in the Lebanese Baccalaureate. Throughout my academic journey, I have always strived for excellence and am passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

As a Computer Science student, I am eager to learn and grow in various fields such as web development, software engineering, and data science. My goal is to create impactful projects that contribute to the tech community and improve people's lives. In my spare time, I enjoy participating in hackathons, exploring new technologies, and sharing my knowledge with others.

Outside of technology, I have a passion for sports and research, which keeps me inspired and balanced. I aim to combine my skills in my field to develop innovative solutions in the future.

"Your only limit is your mind." This quote resonates with me as I strive to push boundaries and embrace challenges. I invite you to connect with me on github to explore my work and share ideas!
